What We Do

Promotion of Standardized Nepalese Superfoods.

Aarvi Digital Nepal, also known as Native Fare, was founded in 2023 to promote standardized Nepalese products like superfoods, highlighting the richness of the country's diverse terrain, including the Himalayas, and its natural resources like herbs, grains, oils, and minerals.

Commitment to Sustainability and Ethical Sourcing

The brand embraces eco-friendly practices throughout its supply chain, partnering with local farmers and communities to promote economic growth and environmental preservation. Ethical sourcing ensures fair compensation for the individuals involved in producing these natural, chemical-free products.

Holistic Approach with Cultural Appreciation

Aarvi Digital Nepal aims to connect consumers worldwide with Nepal's rich cultural heritage and natural bounty by offering not just products, but a holistic experience that intertwines environmental stewardship with respect for the region’s traditions and spiritual heritage.

About Me

Biraj Chaudary,
A dedicated and passionate leader, currently serves as the CEO of Aarvi Digital Nepal, a company committed to promoting Nepalese superfoods and natural products on a global scale. Under his leadership, Aarvi Digital Nepal is thriving by adhering to sustainable practices, ethical sourcing, and environmental stewardship. Biraj's commitment to excellence and his deep respect for Nepal's cultural heritage drive the company's vision of offering standardized, natural, and healthy products that embody the richness of the country's diverse terrain and traditions.
